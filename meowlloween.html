Meowlloween

Meowlloween was a classroom game jam project where I acted as lead artist, designing and creating the characters. This project was a simple black and white point and click adventure, telling the story of a young girl trick or treating in her apartment on halloween night.

Final Concept

Main Character

The main character is a young girl dressed up as a cat for halloween. The character has cute and innocent qualities as she is oblivious do the dangerous neighbours she encounters.

Side Character

Many additional characters were developed from a cute and innocent aesthetic to creepy and ominous. The differently styled character were designed to become progressively unsettling as the main character travels deeper through the halls of her apartment.
