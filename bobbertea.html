Bobber Tea

A simple task to create a logo and identity from a random word and product. This word was “bobber” with the subject “tea”. With an objective to design, with creative freedom, a logo and identity or this fictitious company.

Final Concept

The logo was heavily inspired by the word “bobber”, creating a fishing bobber graphic and following an aquatic/fished theme tea company. The design inside the letters imitating tea showing a wavy and flowy pattern.
